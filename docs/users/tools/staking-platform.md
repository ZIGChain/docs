# ETH and BNB Staking Program (Program Ended)

[ETH and BNB Staking Program](https://staking.zigchain.com/) on ZIGChain concluded on September 29, 2025. 

This program previously allowed users to stake ETH and BNB tokens on the ZIGChain network.
Participants who have not yet withdrawn their funds can still unstake, withdraw, and bridge to ZIGChain to claim their rewards.
