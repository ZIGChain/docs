## What are the account recovery and restoration recommendations?

Use 12–24-word mnemonic backups.  
 For high-value accounts, use multi-signature.  
 Recover using:

`zigchaind keys add --recover`

---

## Is there an important note about MEXC mining deposits?

Note: MEXC exchange addresses must **not** be used for deposits originating from mining activities.  
 _We will inform community channels accordingly._
