## Does ZIGChain support non-native tokens (e.g. USDT)?

Yes. The chain supports non-native/issued tokens (e.g., **USDC, wBTC, ATOM, USDT**), via Token Factory/DEX modules and wrapping/bridging mechanisms.

---

## Is the chain supported by any decentralized exchange providers?

Yes. ZIGChain supports decentralized exchange functionality natively and via the Cosmos ecosystem.

**Native DEX:** ZIGChain includes a protocol-level DEX module, enabling on-chain swaps without relying on external smart contracts.  
Documentation: [https://docs.zigchain.com/builders/dex](https://docs.zigchain.com/builders/dex)

**DEX applications:**

- [https://app.oroswap.org/](https://app.oroswap.org/)
- [https://app.degenter.io/](https://app.degenter.io/)

**IBC-enabled DEX access:** Through IBC (ICS-20), ZIGChain assets can be traded on Cosmos ecosystem DEXs that support IBC-connected chains.

## Is the chain supported by third-party staking providers?

Yes. ZIGChain supports third-party staking providers through its protocol-level staking system.
